Doug's Take

I love the AP1 Honda S2000, as it's a genuinely driver-focused sports car with fantastically balanced handling, a mandatory 6-speed manual transmission, and a high-revving engine. This particular AP1 is a desirable one, as it features a handsome triple-black color combination – and it has only 32,400 miles on the odometer. Plus, this S2000 comes with minimal modifications and only one owner from new – along with a clean, accident-free Carfax report. This looks like an excellent S2000, and I'm sure that its next owner will be thrilled with it.

Highlights

THIS... is a 2000 Honda S2000, finished in Berlina Black with a black soft top and a black interior.

  • This S2000 is equipped with a 6-speed manual transmission, and its odometer currently displays approximately 32,400 miles.
  • The attached Carfax history report contains a gap from 1999 to 2004, though it lists no accidents or mileage discrepancies in the given period; it also shows that this S2000 has been registered to one owner since new.
  • According to the window sticker provided in the gallery, factory equipment includes 16-inch wheels, a limited-slip differential, xenon HID headlights, a power-operated soft top, leather upholstery, a digital instrument cluster, air conditioning, and push-button start. The only modification reported by the seller is a LoJack vehicle tracking system.
  • The Honda S2000, introduced in 1999 for the 2000 model year, was a tribute to Honda's 50th anniversary. Known for its super high-revving 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine and balanced handling, the S2000 quickly gained a reputation as an engaging driver's car.
  • Power comes from a 2.0-liter inline-4, rated at 240 horsepower and 153 lb-ft of torque. Output is sent to the rear wheels via a 6-speed manual transmission.
